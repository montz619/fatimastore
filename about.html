About Fatima Store

Fatima Store is a lightweight storefront demonstrating a mobile-first product catalog for everyday essentials. Product data is stored as JSON in the repository and rendered client-side. Ordering is performed externally (for example, via a messenger app) by copying the cart summary.

Mission

Provide a fast, accessible browsing experience for daily essentials on mobile and desktop.
