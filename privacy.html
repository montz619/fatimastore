Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 23, 2025

Overview

Fatima Store is a small static storefront that showcases food items, school supplies and general merchandise. This site is a static site without user accounts, it does not host or process payments directly. This policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Local cart data: Items you add to the shopping cart are stored locally in your browser (localStorage). We do not send cart contents to any server unless you explicitly submit an order through a checkout flow that integrates with a third-party service.
  • Contact messages: If you use the contact form, the form data may be sent by email or to a third-party form handler depending on configuration. This site does not persist contact form messages on the site itself.
  • Analytics & third parties: This static site does not include analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising by default. If third-party integrations are added (for example for payments or analytics), their own privacy policies will apply.

How we use information

We use information only to support site functionality and customer requests. Examples:

  • Restore your cart contents in your browser during the current session.
  • Respond to contact form submissions via the configured email or form handler.
  • Provide product listings and site features.

Cookies and local storage

This site uses localStorage to save a small amount of data (for example the shopping cart). We do not use cookies for tracking by default. If cookies or other storage are added by third-party providers (for example payments), those providers determine their own cookie behavior.

Third-party services

The site may integrate third-party services for tasks like payments or email. These services are independent controllers of any personal data they collect. Please review their privacy policies before using those features.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information stored on the site. However, because this is a static site, sensitive operations such as payments should be completed through trusted, secure third-party providers.

Your choices

  • You can clear localStorage or your browser data at any time to remove cart information stored locally.
  • If you submitted a contact form and want your data removed, contact us at the address below and we will work to remove it from our records (to the extent we control it).

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, please use the Contact page.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.
